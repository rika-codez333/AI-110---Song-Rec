KEY FINDINGS
============

WHAT FEELS RIGHT ✅
─────────────────
• Primary recommendations are excellent (pop for pop, rock for rock)
• Genre differentiation exists across profiles
• Lofi song clustering is healthy and expected


WHAT FEELS WRONG ⚠️
──────────────────
• Rock songs appear in pop playlists (#4 in High-Energy Pop)
• Massive scoring cliffs (2.15-point drop from #1 to #2 in rock)
• Mood mismatches incur no penalty (asymmetric scoring)
• Energy weight too high (1.4 = 70% of genre weight)


ROOT CAUSE
──────────
1. Energy weight (1.4) enables wrong-genre songs via numeric feature matching
2. Mood penalty asymmetry: match = +1.0, miss = 0.0 (should be -0.5)
3. Small dataset (30 songs, ~3 per genre) limits secondary recommendations


PROPOSED FIX (Option C: Recommended)
────────────────────────────────────

  Feature          Current → Proposed
  ──────────────────────────────────
  Genre            2.0 → 2.3
  Mood (miss)      0.0 → -0.5 (NEW)
  Energy           1.4 → 1.2

Expected outcome:
  • Storm Runner drops from #4 to #7-10 in pop recommendations
  • Sunrise City stays at #1 (minimal score impact)
  • Genre coherence improves without sacrificing feature-based discovery
